PROPERTY VALUE GUIDE — LOCAL-EVIDENCE CORRECTION — 16 JULY 2026
================================================================

- Removed the defective Melbourne/regional fallback that ignored the suburb
  entered by the user and could materially overstate a property's value.
- Added official Valuer-General Victoria 2025 annual and October–December 2025
  house/unit sales medians for 809 Victorian localities.
- The calculator now finds an exact suburb and property-type series, weights
  the annual and latest-quarter evidence by the published sales count, and
  shows the local figures used in the result.
- If no matching local series, verified independent median or comparable sale
  exists, the calculator stops instead of inventing a price.
- Matching comparable sales are blended with the local median; extreme sales
  outside 25% of the comparable centre are excluded when two valid sales remain.
- Reduced and capped feature, condition, timing and comparable adjustments so
  optional answers cannot compound into an unrealistic uplift.
- Townhouses are clearly identified as a local house/unit model because the
  official tables do not publish a separate townhouse series.
- Older saved estimates are recalculated with the corrected engine before they
  are shown, and the offline cache now includes the local sales dataset.
- Kept the existing black, white and yellow theme, Project save/reopen, notes,
  attachments, follow-up tasks and print support.
