Support

Fast checks for sign-in, email links, iPhone uploads, AI analysis and role permissions.

Email verification

  1. Open the newest verification email; older links may be expired.
  2. Use the same browser where practical.
  3. The link must return to Alert Construction → Secure Account.
  4. If it does not, use “Resend verification email” on the sign-up page.

iPhone plan or quote upload

  1. Choose the file from Photos or Files.
  2. Use PDF/JPG where possible. HEIC photos are converted in the browser when Safari supports the source.
  3. Plans must be under 7 MB each; quotes under 12 MB.
  4. If Safari reloads, export a reduced-size PDF and retry on a stable connection.

AI did not finish

  • Session expired: sign in again.
  • Busy: wait briefly and retry; the selected file remains in place.
  • Quota/configuration: contact the app Owner; the server secret or billing limit needs attention.
  • Incomplete result: retry with a clearer, smaller PDF and check that the correct trade sheets and legends are included.

Saving and PDF permissions

Owner/Admin and Builder/Estimator roles can save project records, estimate/quote records, backups and PDFs. Site Supervisors can open and use tools in view-only mode. An Owner changes roles from Secure Account.

Account deletion is available in Secure Account → Delete account.

Privacy and screenshots

The app watermarks protected pages, covers the content when the browser is backgrounded, and blocks in-app printing for unauthorised roles. A website cannot technically prevent every operating-system screenshot or an external camera. Do not display sensitive plans or client details to an unauthorised person.

Contact Support

Ask your Alert Construction workspace Owner to configure a monitored support email before production. Include the page name, time, device/browser, and the exact error text. Do not email passwords, API keys, full plans or unnecessary client information.
